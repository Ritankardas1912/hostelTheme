About

Radhakrishnan Bhawan (B.T. Men's) is one of the official hostels of Kalyani University. Situated in the mid city province, it has access to all the necessary places within walking distance. One the safest and cleanest hostels of Kalyani University.

  • Total accommodation space for boarders is 120.
  • Each boarder have their personal table, chair and locker space for storing personal items.
  • Out of 33 rooms, one is taken by official, two by cooking staff and one is kept as a sick room.

Food for more!!

Our hostel provides super hygiene food for two times a day. Cooking staffs are there for cooking and serving.

Food is provided for two times a day, ie. morning and night. Plates and other utensils are also provided by hostel, Boarder's need not bring any kind of utensils unless he has a certain problem. Food in made in very hygiene condition maintained by cooking staffs who are supper conscious about the hygiene of the food being made.

Ground for more!!

Our hostel has great ground for all the boarders to play and refresh their minds.

  • A full size cricket, volleyball, badminton ground is present.
  • Indoor playing equipments like carrom, chess, table tennis are also their for boarders.
  • All playing equipments are provided by hostel at no extra charges.

24 X 7 ambulance and hospital service

24 x 7 Ambulance service especially reserved for boarders.

Although hostel has medical support and hospitals at walking distance, still 24 x 7 ambulance service is there for any emergencies. If called ambulance will be at the gate within 2 minutes of calling.
